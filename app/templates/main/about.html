URL Shortener

URL Shortener is an amazing tool. You can shorten your long urls to enhance your productivity.

Simply go to homepage and enter your long url in 'URL' field.If you wish to use custom back-half URL, simply enter your custom back-half string in 'custom back-half' field. However this is an optional step. If you wish, you can leave 'custom back-half' field empty. Then URL Shortener will automatically generate a short URL consisting of upper-case and lower-case letters and numbers. Length of automatically generated URL will be on first come first serve basis. The URL shortened first will have small length short URL than late ones.
